Giving Back

We are always searching for ways to give back to our community. Last November we raised more than $1300 to donate to New Life. In addition to the monetary donation, we continue to donate furniture and household goods from our jobs. The money and furniture go toward assisting families and individuals transitioning out of homelessness. This was critical, more than ever, right before the cold winter months rolled around.
